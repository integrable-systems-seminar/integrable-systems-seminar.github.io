Nordic remote HET seminars

INTRODUCTION

Welcome to the joint Nordic remote HET seminar initiative! The seminars will take place on Zoom, the tentative schedule can be found below. Talks will be scheduled on Tuesdays, 14h15 CET as much as possible. Each seminar will be hosted by one of the local organizers of the participating institutes, and further information (including abstracts) can be found on their respective websites.

SCHEDULE

The talks are password-protected, contact one of the local organizers to gain access.

For the abstract of a talk, please see the website of the listed host institute, which are given below. We warmly invite researchers from participating institutes to send us names of potential speakers.
